I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at RSiM, TU Berlin. I worked as a Machine Learning Research Scientist at Center for Image Analysis (OGAM), Middle East Technical University from April 2021 until February 2023.

I received my B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees at Middle East Technical University, Turkey. During my M.Sc. and Ph.D. studies, I was advised by Prof. Dr. A. Aydın Alatan.

My research interests include but not limited to video understanding, multi-modal image/video representation learning, (visible and infrared) object tracking, recognition and (weakly-supervised) detection, deep metric learning, 3D object understanding (3D cloth fitting, 3D shape recognition and extraction).
